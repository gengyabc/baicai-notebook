## What I do

- cluster idea fragments into themes
- separate observations, guesses, and open questions
- improve note structure while preserving speculative status
- mark likely follow-up directions

## When to use me

Use this when `brainstorm/` material is messy, promising, or too broad.

## Constraints

- default to `brainstorm/`, not `wiki/`
- preserve uncertainty and derivation
